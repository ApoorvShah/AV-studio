INTERIOR DESIGNER / 3D Visualizer

ATHARV SHAH

After accumulating more than two years of experience in the field, I am a freelancer that is eager to collaborate with fellow architects and interior designers to actualize their design concepts by utilizing my Interior design and 3D visualization skills. With my expertise, I am able to generate photo-realistic rendered images that bring their visions to life

Specializing in professional interior design, residential and commercial premises. Professional approach. Individual design of houses, apartments. Author’s supervision. Implementation of projects from idea to final result. Selection of ecological finishing materials, furniture, textiles, office equipment and accessories.
